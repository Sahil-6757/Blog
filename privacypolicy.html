The information we collect is used to: Provide you with news content and updates. Personalize your experience on the website. Improve our website based on user feedback and analytics. Our website may use "cookies" to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your computer’s hard drive that track information about your interactions with our website. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, disabling cookies may limit your access to certain features of the site.

6. Data Security

7. Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to: Access and update your personal information. Unsubscribe from any communications or newsletters at any time. Request the deletion of your personal information where applicable.
